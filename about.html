BE MY A11Y started with a simple conviction: that inclusive design isn't a feature — it's a foundation. Here's how we got here, and where we're going.

How We Started

A problem worth solving

BE MY A11Y was founded on the belief that too many businesses were leaving people behind — not out of malice, but because no one had shown them a better way. Every inaccessible website is a door slammed in someone's face.

We set out to change that. Starting with small businesses and growing from there, BE MY A11Y became a trusted partner for organizations that wanted to do right by every person who visited their digital front door.

Today, we combine that original mission with the power of modern AI — making accessibility faster, smarter, and more achievable than ever before.

Our Strategy

Web inclusivity is our foundation. By removing technical barriers we strengthen your online presence, expand your market, and ensure every visitor — regardless of ability or background — can navigate with ease.

Our Mission

Inclusive design benefits all people. Society functions at optimal efficiency when individuals of all backgrounds and limitations have equal access to information. Education, equity, and engagement — that's what we build toward.

Our Commitment

Every AI capability we adopt serves one goal: making the digital world more open to everyone. We review every automated finding with human expertise — because context matters, and people matter more than process.
